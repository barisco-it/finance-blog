# Homepage Wireframe – SadeFinans

This homepage follows a clean minimal blog structure
with subtle educational positioning.

No marketing tone.
No banners.
No aggressive calls to action.

---

## 1. Header

Left:
SadeFinans (text logo)

Right:
Yazılar | Hakkında

Rules:
- Minimal spacing
- No dropdown menus
- No category clutter
- Future: optional Bülten link

---

## 2. Minimal Hero Section

Purpose:
Immediately communicate positioning without feeling corporate.

### Headline

Finansı sade ve akılcı şekilde anlamak.

### Subtext (2 lines max)

SadeFinans, finansal konuları abartısız ve sistematik şekilde anlatır.
Uzun vadeli düşünmeyi ve reel getiriyi merkeze alır.

Rules:
- No background image
- No gradient
- No call-to-action button
- Simple typography
- Generous whitespace

---

## 3. Latest Articles Section

Title:
Son Yazılar

Layout:
Vertical article cards (clean, not heavy boxes)

Each card includes:
- Title
- Short excerpt (2–3 lines)
- Publish date
- Up to 3 visible tags (minimal styling)

Rules:
- No thumbnails initially (optional later)
- Focus on readability
- Clean typography hierarchy

---

## 4. Highlighted Tags Section

Purpose:
Subtle structural guidance.

Display 4–5 core tags as small buttons:

- finansal-okuryazarlik
- uzun-vade
- risk
- turkiye
- nasil-yapilir

Rules:
- Minimal styling
- No tag cloud
- No overwhelming list
- Acts as topic exploration entry point

---

## 5. Footer

Contains:
- Short mission sentence
- Disclaimer link
- Copyright
- (Future: newsletter)

Tone:
Calm and understated.

---

# Design Philosophy

- Clean minimal blog surface
- Structured educational backbone
- Rational friend tone
- No hype elements
- No marketing pressure
- No performance signaling

---

# What We Avoid

- Banner ads
- Popups
- Aggressive CTAs
- Category-heavy navigation
- Sidebar clutter
- Stock market ticker widgets

Simplicity is strategic.
